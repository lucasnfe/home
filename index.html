About

I'm broadly interested in the intersections between Machine Learning and Creativity, specifically in algorithmic music composition and procedural content generation for games. I'm currently working on controlling generative deep learning models to compose music with a given emotion. I received a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of California, Santa Cruz (UCSC), a M.Sc. in Computer Science from the Universidade de São Paulo (USP), and a B.Sc. in Computer Science from Universidade Federal do Paraná (UFPR).
