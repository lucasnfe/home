Course Schedule

Below is the Stanford Pre-Collegiate Summer Institute 2017 course schedule (3 weeks).

The first week is focused on programming in Lua and game design. In the second week, the students are presented to techniques for modeling physics and Non-player Characters (NPCs). Finally, in the third week, they are introduced to computer graphics, especialy methods for creating 2D visual effects and for generating visual content.

The creative project proposal is due at the end of the first week (Fri 7/21).
