VGMIDI Dataset

VGMIDI is a dataset of 200 MIDI labelled piano pieces (966 phrases of 4 bars) and 728 non-labelled ones, all piano arrangements of video game soundtracks. Each piece was annotated by 30 human subjects according to a valence-arousal (dimensional) model of emotion. The sentiment of each piece was then extracted by summarizing the 30 annotations and mapping the valence axis to sentiment. The dataset is available on github.
